Stains

"It's that easy, Shin!"

Meet Milo and Shin!!! In this episode, Milo shows us how to get rid of a stain on your shirt. Who knew you could do this WITHOUT a Mr. Clean Magic Eraser!

I found this life hack very useful back in grade school. From age 6-18, I had attended a private christian school with stuff teachers and equally stuffy uniforms. They would not take it lightly if you came to school with a stain in what should be a pristine white shirt (one you would have to wear 3-4 days a week!). Those standards were EXTREME for a small, slightly unkept munchkin like myself.

Luckily, I had found this hack in middle school and it changed my life. I hope this helps you!
