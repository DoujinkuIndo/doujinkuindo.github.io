## <center>Bruin Feed</center>{: .acm-blue }

### <center>User friendly dining app for UCLA Bruins</center>{: .acm-cerulean }

![_config.yml]({{ site.baseurl }}/images/showcase/bruin-feed-screenshots.png){: .center-image }

##### <center>Summary: Bruin Feed is a dining app built for UCLA's dining halls. It contains all menu items, nutrition information, the ability to track your food stats, and favorite your favorite dishes. With this cleanly designed user interface, bruins can always know what's on the menu.</center>
