title: "Enabled backchannel: conference Twitter use by digital humanists" 
author: C. Ross, M. Terras, C. Warwick, A. Welsh
topic: This paper considers the use of Twitter as a digital backchannel by the Digital Humanities (DH) community. 
question: Does the use of a twitter-enabled back channel enhance the conference experience, collaboration, and the co-construction of knowledge?
significance: This paper is the first known exhaustive study that concentrates on how microblogging technologies such as Twitter are used by and can benefit scholars.
