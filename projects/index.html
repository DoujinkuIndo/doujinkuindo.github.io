ZOMBIE ATTACK GAME

Yet another zombie attack Javascript Game - Your city is under attack by Zombies and your mission is to kill them all. Simply enter a row letter and a column number (e.g. A0) and press FIRE. You have unlimited ammo and time.

Also some tips for you. Zombies like to wander in groups of three. And the console if your best friend.
