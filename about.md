Cheryl Hung ([@oicheryl](http://oicheryl.com/)) is VP Ecosystem at the [Cloud Native Computing Foundation](https://www.cncf.io/), where she leads the [CNCF End User Community](https://www.cncf.io/people/end-user-community/). At over 140 members, including global names such as Apple, Spotify and Mastercard, it is the largest end user community of any open source foundation or standards body. CNCF is home to over 70 of the fastest growing open source infrastructure projects, including Kubernetes, Prometheus and Envoy, as part of the Linux Foundation.
