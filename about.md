I am an engineer and (data) science enthusiast. I am interested in understanding how learning processes can uncover hidden mechanics of the universe, be that through data science, complex systems, or energy dynamics.

I joined [Tomorrow](https://www.tmrow.com/) in March 2020, where I work as a data scientist for [ElectricityMap](https://app.electricitymap.org/map) to find scalable solutions to climate change.

* I have a dual Masters degree in general engineering and applied mathematics from [Ecole CentraleSupélec](https://www.centralesupelec.fr/) and [Denmark's Technical University](https://www.dtu.dk/), where I completed my Masters thesis on robust uncertainty quantification using out-of-distribution pseudo-inputs training under the supervision of [Søren Hauberg](http://www2.compute.dtu.dk/~sohau/).
