Cheryl Hung is Director of Ecosystem at the Cloud Native Computing Foundation. As a non-profit under the Linux Foundation, the CNCF hosts Kubernetes, Prometheus, Envoy and many other open source projects. Her mission is to drive adoption of cloud native infrastructure.

She founded the Cloud Native London meetup and is Chair of the upcoming Kubernetes Community Days London conference. She holds a Masters in Computer Science from the University of Cambridge, King's College, and previously wrote C++ as a software engineer at Google.
