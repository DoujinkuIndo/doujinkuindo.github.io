I am the Director of Ecosystem at the Cloud Native Computing Foundation, part of the Linux Foundation.

I speak frequently at meetups and conferences on container and cloud native technologies, and founded the [Cloud Native London meetup](https://www.meetup.com/Cloud-Native-London). It's dedicated to building a strong, open, diverse developer community around the Cloud Native platform and technologies in London.
