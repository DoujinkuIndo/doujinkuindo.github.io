Cheryl Hung is VP Ecosystem at the [Cloud Native Computing Foundation](https://www.cncf.io/), part of the Linux Foundation and home to over 70 fast growing open source projects including Kubernetes, Prometheus and Envoy. She leads the 140+ members of the [CNCF End User Community](https://www.cncf.io/people/end-user-community/), the largest end user community of any open source foundation or standards body. 

Cheryl is a [frequent speaker](http://oicheryl.com/) and founder of the [Cloud Native London meetup](http://cloudnativelon.com/). As a C++ engineer she previously worked on backend features on Google Maps, and holds a Masters in Computer Science from the University of Cambridge, Kingâ€™s College.
