Cheryl Hung is VP Ecosystem at the [Cloud Native Computing Foundation](https://www.cncf.io/), part of the Linux Foundation and home to over 70 of the fastest growing open source infrastructure projects, including Kubernetes, Prometheus and Envoy. She leads of the [CNCF End User Community](https://www.cncf.io/people/end-user-community/) including leading global technology companies such as Apple, Spotify and Mastercard. At over 140 members, it is the largest end user community of any open source foundation or standards body. 

Cheryl is a [regulary keynote speaker](http://oicheryl.com/) and founder of the [Cloud Native London meetup](http://cloudnativelon.com/).
