DistNet

DistNet is a distributed convolution neural network. It takes cuda-conv as it's underlying convolution operations GPU-implementation and uses MPI as the communication interface. Â DistNet is able to scale to multiple GPUs to accelerate the training procedure. We tested on 4 GPUs with Alex's network and achieved 2.57x times speedup. And we are optimistic to believe that we could speedup more while scaling to 9 or 16 GPUs.

Thanks to Russell Power. Worked heavily with this great man.

Cell

Cell is a distributed Btree built on top of infiniband. With infiniband's low latency, high speed and kernel bypass capability, Cell is a remarkably fast distributed Btree.
