Veganism

I am vegan since since the beginning of 2017 after one moment of clear thinking. I had the courage and the decancy to confront the hard truth - I was participating in needless acts of violence against animals for the sake of my own pleasure and convenience.

The animals of the world exist for their own reasons. They were not made for humans any more than black people were made for white, or women created for men.

I invite you to watch the videos down below and make your choice.

Earthlings (Documentary)

EARTHLINGS is a feature-length documentary about humankind's absolute economic dependence on animals raised as pets, food, clothing, entertainment and scientific research. Using hidden cameras and never-before-seen footage, EARTHLINGS chronicles the day-to-day practices at some of the largest industries in the world, all of which rely entirely on animals for profit.
