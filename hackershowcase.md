## <center>Hacker Showcase</center>{: .acm-blue }

<center>This is an area to showcase all the projects that past and current UCLA students have built.
We are hoping to foster a community by sharing our experiences, and hopefully inspiring
the next generation of hackers.</center>

## <center>How to submit a project</center>{: .acm-blue }
<center>
	Want to add your own project to the collection? Simply fill out this <a href="https://goo.gl/forms/r957uTzE0jdafQkd2"> survey</a> and we will review your project. If you don't receive a reply after 1 week, please email Akhil Nadendla: <a href="mailto:anadendla@ucla.edu?Subject=Hacker Showcase Concern" target="_top">anadendla@ucla.edu</a>
</center>
