BMW Dealership's Kiosk app

BMW’s biggest dealer in Peru was looking to increase its lead generation by modernizing its process of creating quotes. I was responsible for designing a solution and leading its development.

The result of the consultation was an iPad kiosk app that would be placed close to exhibits and that could make extremely easy for customers to check car’s features, play with configurations and getting a quote without talking to a seller.

Roles

  • Sole Product Design
  • Lead iOS Developer

The Problem

Gildemeister S.A, BMW’s leading car dealer in Peru, was dealing with a year over year decrease in car sales. One key goal for the year was to modernize the experience inside their stores.

I conducted interviews with 3 sellers, 2 IT employees, and spent a day making quick surveys to real customers visiting the showrooms.
