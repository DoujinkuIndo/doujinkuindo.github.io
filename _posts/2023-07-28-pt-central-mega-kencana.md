**PT Central Mega Kencana** has established its presence for 50 years in the world of luxury as the biggest retail jewelry company in Southeast Asia, with over 110 stores. CMK caters the customers from all characteristics through the company’s subsidiaries—MONDIAL, Frank & co., and The Palace Jeweler. CMK employs over 1,200 skilled jewelry craftsmen at CMK Studio and 2,500 employees across Indonesia. Through the establishment of CMK club in 2018, CMK has acquired more than 730,000 loyal jewelry enthusiasts.
