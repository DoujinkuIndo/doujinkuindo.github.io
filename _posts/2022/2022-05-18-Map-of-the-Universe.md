Understanding your team's scope of responsibility is made easier with a Map of the Universe. This one-page diagram outlines the high-level applications your team maintains and how data flows between them.

![Map of the universe](https://johnuhri.com/y0mbo.github.io/images/2022/mapOfTheUniverse.png)

The map is particularly beneficial to orient new teammates during onboarding.

I keep our map up-to-date with changes to show our progress as new applications replace legacy systems. It's rewarding to see the successes we've had.
