Join us at the June Cloud Native London virtual meetup tonight on Rambly and YouTube to hear three fantastic speakers from Shipa, Suade Labs, and Kasten by Veeam, from 6:45pm BST!

[Join us on Rambly](https://rambly.app/#/rooms/twitch:cloudnativelon) or catch the Youtube livestream.

### Our agenda for 2nd June 2021 is (times in BST):

* 6:45 Kick off
* 7:15 Developers do not need Kubernetes (Viktor Farcic, Shipa)
* 7:45 Software License and DevOps (Hubert Bosiacki, Suade Labs)
* 8:30 Kubestr: Identify, Validate and Evaluate your Kubernetes Storage (Michael Cade, Kasten by Veeam)
* 9:00 Wrap up

We are immensely grateful to [Tecknuovo](https://www.tecknuovo.com/), [Humio](https://humio.com/), [Palo Alto Networks](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/), [CircleCI](https://circleci.com/), [UMATR](https://www.umatr.io/), and [Kasten by Veeam](https://www.kasten.io/veeam) for your generous support and sponsorship.
