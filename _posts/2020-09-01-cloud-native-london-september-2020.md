Join us at the September Cloud Native London virtual meetup with speakers from Redis Labs, Suade Labs, and Oracle, from 6:30pm BST on Zoom and YouTube!

[Join us on Zoom](https://zoom.us/my/cherylhung) using the password: 'cheryl' or catch the livestream via Youtube.

### Our agenda for 1st September 2020 is (times in BST):

* 6:45 Kick off
* 7:15 Rediscover Redis: Beyond Cache (Pieter Cailliau, Redis Labs)
* 7:45 How to not kubernetes: old habits in container world (Hubert Bosiacki, Suade Labs)
* 8:15 Break
* 8:30 Bring your code and not Kubernetes YAML (Prasenjit Sarkar, Oracle)
* 9:00 Wrap up
