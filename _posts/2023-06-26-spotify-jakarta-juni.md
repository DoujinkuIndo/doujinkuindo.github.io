Berdasarkan informasi di laman [Karir Spotify](https://www.lifeatspotify.com/jobs), terdapat lowongan kerja sebagai berikut:

## Indonesia Communications Lead, SEA Communications

We are looking for a new band member to lead Communications - Corporate, B2B, Consumer & Influencer PR - for Indonesia, a key market in one of Spotify’s fastest growing regions. The successful candidate will be a strategic problem solver and a hands-on implementer, leading proactive and reactive communications across our earned and owned editorial channels. We are also looking for a strong storyteller who can demonstrate both traditional, as well as non-traditional channels to amplify the Spotify brand and apply perception to build FOMO with key audiences in the market.
This role will be a direct report to Spotify’s Head of Communications for SEA who is based in Singapore.

### What You'll Do

* Lead both external and internal communications for Indonesia, covering all aspects of our business - including creator (music & podcasts), consumer, corporate, industry (music and advertising), crisis and employee communications
* Regularly engage with and drive buy-in with stakeholders across the business, including leading as trusted communications counsel for our Spotify Indonesia team, and more broadly to the cross-functional SEA and global communications teams
* Manage external agencies (across PR and influencer), setting strategy while ensuring efficiencies of time and resources, and maximising business impact
* Be adept at leading multiple projects and engagements that focus on thought leadership and creating talkability for the brand
* Work across functional partners - global, regional and local - to ensure that messaging themes for Indonesia are consistent across all external facing channels
* Build and maintain positive relationships with key media and influencer contacts in the market, i.e., be the face of Spotify Indonesia to these targets
* Work from and interact with team in-person from our office in Jakarta
* Lead any issues and crisis communications

### Who You Are

* You have more than 10 years relevant communications experience in media, digital, entertainment, tech or music industry comms
* Proficient in English and Bahasa Indonesia - outstanding writing and communications skills in both a must
* Proven track record of developing and executing strategic PR & influencer campaigns, with experience with creatively working with homeless/digital media and existing relationships with influencers/KOLs a plus
* Motivated self-starter with hands-on attitude; happy to embrace a bit of chaos
* Ability to multitask and work in a hectic and extremely fast-moving industry and environment, without sacrificing attention to detail
* Collaborative individual who enjoys partnering closely across teams and departments to build and distribute the most impactful communications possible
* Strong project management and agency management skills, with ability to balance short- and long-term priorities
* Ability to translate sophisticated concepts into clear communication
* Strong strategic problem solver and resourceful problem-solver
* Great teammate who thinks locally, regionally and globally
* Ability to work across time zones and travel domestically and internationally
* Of course, an ever-enthusiastic representative of the Spotify brand!

### Our global benefits

* **Extensive learning opportunities**, through our dedicated team, GreenHouse.
* **Flexible share incentives** letting you choose how you share in our success.
* **Global parental leave**, six months off - fully paid - for all new parents.
* **All The Feels**, our employee assistance program and self-care hub.
* **Flexible public holidays**, swap days off according to your values and beliefs.
