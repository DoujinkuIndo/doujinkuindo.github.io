Our August Cloud Native London virtual meetup kicks off at 6:30pm BST, join us and speakers from Dynatrace, Contino, and Microsoft on Zoom and YouTube!

[Join us on Zoom](https://zoom.us/my/cherylhung) using the password: 'cheryl' or catch the livestream via Youtube.

### Our agenda for 5 August 2020 is (times in BST):

* 6:45 Kick off
* 7:15 Cloud Native Apps for Inferencing and Analytics in Telco Edge Cloud (Anurag Ranjan, Intel)
* 7:45 Helping traditional security teams become Cloud Native (Stuart Wyatt, Palo Alto Networks)
* 8:15 Break
* 8:30 Hyper scaling Webex media on CloudNative (Hasith Kalpage and Mercion Wilathgamuwage, Cisco)
* 9:00 Wrap up
