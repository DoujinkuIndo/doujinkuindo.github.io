1.6.2 Dropout

efficient , reduce the complex co-adapations of neurals, since a neural cannot rely on a particular neural cost factor of 2 to make a combination

  • at train time

setting ot zero in a probablity of 0.5 , if set to 0, don't participant the feed forward and back propagation.

  • at test time

use all the neural, but multiply their output by 0.5, approximate the dropout effect. reduce overfitting , reduce about twice iterations to coverage
