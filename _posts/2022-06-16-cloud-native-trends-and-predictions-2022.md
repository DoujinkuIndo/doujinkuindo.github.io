At Cloud First Edinburgh, I made ten predictions for cloud native in 2022. Check them out here!

### 10 predictions for cloud native in 2022

🛠 Productivity: reduce complexity by moving up the stack
1. Better experience for dev & ops
1. Cross Cloud becomes real
1. GitOps grows significantly
1. Jenkins is not dead

👩🏻‍💻 Control: tools for better insights, enabling optimisation
1. eBPF for sidecar-less service mesh
1. More Rust in Cloud Native
1. Shift Left empowers devs

🌐 Business - where business meets cloud, what to prioritise in a world of limited resources
1. Developer Advocates everywhere
1. Rise of FinOps
1. GreenOps drives resource efficiency
