Itsycal for Mac

Download Itsycal for OS X 10.10+

Itsycal is a tiny calendar for your Mac's menu bar.

If you want, it will display your calendar events as a companion to the Mac Calendar app. You can also create and delete (but not edit) events.

•   •   •

On OS X 10.10 Yosemite (but not 10.11 El Capitan*), you can move Itsycal in the menu bar just like the built-in Apple menu extras by holding down the ⌘ key and dragging.

•   •   •

Most of Itsycal's features can be controlled with the keyboard.

left/right arrows or H/L previous/next month
up/down arrows or K/J previous/next year
space today
shift+arrows or h,j,k,l move selection
p pin/unpin Itsycal
w toggle calendar weeks
⌘, preferences
⌘N create a new event
⌘O open default calendar app
⌘Q quit

 

History & Previous Versions

 

* Itsycal cannot move in the menu bar on OS X 10.11 because of changes made by Apple for security reasons. However, all hope is not lost. If you want to move Itsycal on 10.11, there is a clever app called Bartender that somehow manages to allow all of your menu bar items to move. ↩
