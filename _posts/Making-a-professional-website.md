As a student or early career researcher, you may have seen a trend professional websites created by your peers. You may have wondered whether or not you, yourself, should make one. In this post, I will give you a couple of reasons on why you should make a website and give a tutorial on how to do so using GitHub Pages.

## Why make a website?

- Sharing your research. High light your work, advcoate yourself

- Interactive CV. Showase other parts of CV

- Provide Resources, protocols, code, data, papers

- Contact info, general information

- Keep track of your progress

- Be foudn. Improves visibility

- Complete control on how you are presented. COntrol the message
