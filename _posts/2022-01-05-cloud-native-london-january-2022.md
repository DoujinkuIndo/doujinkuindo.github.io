Kicking off 2022 at Cloud Native London, three great speakers from Komodor, Livecycle, and Pulumi join us at the Cloud Native London virtual meetup tonight, from 6:45pm GMT on Rambly and YouTube! Hubert will be hosting.

[Join us on Rambly](https://rambly.app/#/rooms/twitch:cloudnativelon) or catch the Youtube livestream.

### Our agenda for 5 January 2022 is (times in GMT):

* 6:45 Kick off
* 7:15 Why DevOps Tools Do Not Speak Developer Language - And How To Overcome This (Itiel Shwartz, Komodor)
* 7:45 Experimenting with Deno for Easier Kubernetes Deployments (Yshay Yaacobi, Livecycle)
* 8:15 Break
* 8:30 Klustered: Debugging Kubernetes Live (David Flanagan, Pulumi)
* 9:00 Wrap up

We are immensely grateful to [Humio](https://humio.com/), [Palo Alto Networks](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/), and [CircleCI](https://circleci.com/), for your generous support and sponsorship.
