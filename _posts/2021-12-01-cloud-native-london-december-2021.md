We have two fantastic speakers from Ondat and VMWare joining the December Cloud Native London virtual meetup tonight, from 6:45pm GMT on Rambly and YouTube! Hubert will be hosting again.

[Join us on Rambly](https://rambly.app/#/rooms/twitch:cloudnativelon) or catch the Youtube livestream.

### Our agenda for 1 December 2021 is (times in GMT):

* 6:45 Kick off
* 7:15 How to optimize and scale cloud-native application storage (Rodney Karemba, Ondat)
* 7:45 Understanding design, performance and benchmarking of Kubernetes (Dean Lewis, VMWare)
* 8:15 Wrap up

We are immensely grateful to [Humio](https://humio.com/), [Palo Alto Networks](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/), [CircleCI](https://circleci.com/), [Netapp](https://www.netapp.com/), and [Ondat](https://www.ondat.io/) for your generous support and sponsorship.
