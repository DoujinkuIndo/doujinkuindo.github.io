* Data centers emit as much CO2 as the airline industry. If the global IT industry were a country, only China and the United States would contribute more to climate change.

* We can improve utilization significantly through Kubernetes bin-packing and multi-tenancy capabilities. This means using less energy and resources to run the same workloads.

* Global internet traffic is more than doubling every four years. With increasing video, Edge, IoT, etc, and with large populations in China, India and other countries coming online, this trend looks set to continue.
