**PT Serasi Autoraya (SERA)** adalah anak perusahaan PT Astra International Tbk yang saat ini telah dikenal luas sebagai salah satu perusahaan layanan transportasi dan logistik terbesar di Indonesia.
![logo sera]({{ site.baseurl }}/images/logo-sera.png)

SERA yang pada awalnya perusahaan jasa penyewaan kendaraan, kini telah menjelma menjadi perusahaan penyedia transportasi yang terbesar dan terdepan. Profil perusahaan selengkapnya [baca di sini](https://www.sera.astra.co.id/id/profile).

---

Berdasarkan informasi di laman [Karir SERA Astra](https://career.sera.astra.co.id/), terdapat lowongan kerja terbaru sebagai berikut:

## Sales Consultant

### Job Descriptions:

- Coordinate the sales offer process to prospective customers
- Coordinate in negotiating with customers
- Prospecting potential customers
- Analyze and calculate the selling price given to the customer
- Prepare administrative requirements for dealing transactions
- Manage transactions for each prospective customer from the offer process to cash in
- Coordinate with operations related to customer's goods delivery needs
- Sales monitoring carried out by sales officers to meet targets
- Make sales achievement reports and pending billing

### Job Requirements: 

- Candidate at least bachelor degree from any field
- Having at least 2 year(s) experience in sales in logistic field will be an advantage
- Good communication skill, passionate in sales 
- Enthusiast to work by target.
- Placement in Semarang

**Close Date: 16/08/2023**
