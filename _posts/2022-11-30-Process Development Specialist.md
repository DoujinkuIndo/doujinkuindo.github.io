**Suntory Garuda Beverage (SGB)** merupakan salah satu perusahaan minuman terbesar di Indonesia.
![logo suntory garuda]({{ site.baseurl }}/images/new-logo-sgb.png)

Perusahaan induk SGB adalah Grup Suntory yang berkantor pusat di Tokyo, Jepang yang merupakan perusahaan multinasional dan pemimpin industri minuman di dunia.

SGB memproduksi beragam produk minuman, mulai dari minuman jelly Okky; teh siap minum Mountea; teh botol MYTEA dan air rasa kebaikan alami - Good Mood. Profil perusahaan selengkapnya dapat dibaca [di sini](https://www.suntorygaruda.com/informasi-perusahaan).

## Kualifikasi:

- Having Min. Bachelors Degree (S1) from Chemistry, Food Technology, Food Science, Nutrition, Industry or related scientific field;

- Having 2 (two) years of working experiences in Research and Development;

- Strong in beverage processing development (formulation, flow process, shelf life study);

- Having experience working in F&B industry is an advantage;

- Fluent in English;

## Tanggung jawab:

- Conducts lab trial and scale up trial for exist product improvement, rejuvenate product and new product development.

- Develop and validate formulation, production process, evaluating raw and key material, equipment and final product.

- Collaborate with external and internal parties for trial, scale up and commercial production.

- Analyse data and maintain good documentation of all phases related to research and development.

- Conduct routine experiments/analysis that may improve application process and key material advantage.

- Develop solution and provide technical support for product and process development in All Suntory Garuda business Unit.
