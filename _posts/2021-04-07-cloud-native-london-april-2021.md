Join us at the April Cloud Native London virtual meetup tonight to hear three fantastic speakers from JFrog, StorageOS, and Snyk, on Rambly and YouTube from 6:45pm BST!

[Join us on Rambly](https://rambly.app/#/rooms/twitch:cloudnativelon) or catch the Youtube livestream.

### Our agenda for 7th April 2021 is (times in BST):

* 6:45 Kick off
* 7:15 CI/CD Pipeline for Kubernetes (Batel Tova, JFrog)
* 7:45 Performance and Benchmarking Cloud Native Storage (Alex Chircop, StorageOS)
* 8:15 Break
* 8:30 Continuous Security - Building Security into your Pipelines (Matt Jarvis, Snyk)
* 9:00 Wrap up
