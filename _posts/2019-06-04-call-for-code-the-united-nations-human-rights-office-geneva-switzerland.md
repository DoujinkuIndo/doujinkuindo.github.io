I was invited to Geneva, Switzerland to take part in a hackathon with the United Nations Human Rights Office.

![Cheryl Hung](/images/2019-06-04-callforcode1.jpg)

![Cheryl Hung](/images/2019-06-04-callforcode2.jpg)

Our challenge was to come up with innovative technology to ensure that following a natural disaster, the elderly, disabled and impoverished receive equal access to humanitarian action and assistance.

![Cheryl Hung](/images/2019-06-04-callforcode3.jpg)

![Cheryl Hung](/images/2019-06-04-callforcode4.jpg)

Apart from myself representing the Linux Foundation, other participants included IBM, Capgemini, Red Hat, Deutsche Telekom, Lloyds and RBS.
