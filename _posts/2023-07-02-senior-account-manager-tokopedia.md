Berdasarkan informasi di laman [Karir Tokopedia](https://www.tokopedia.com/careers/jobs), terdapat lowongan kerja terbaru sebagai berikut:

## Senior Account Manager - Ads Solutions

### Description

Tokopedia is seeking an ambitious, experienced, and client-focused Account Manager to join our new and rapidly growing marketing solutions team. This is a unique opportunity to shape our branding solutions business from the ground up. In this role, you will leverage your deep understanding of digital advertising and branding to forge solid relationships with top marketing executives and deliver our upper-funnel growth solutions.

If you're passionate about marketing, thrive in building senior relationships, and possess a deep understanding of the digital advertising landscape, this role is your ticket to making a significant impact on the global stage. Here's your chance to help brands realize their potential while driving growth for one of the world's most influential tech giants.

### What You Will Do

- Cultivate and manage relationships with key marketing stakeholders & decision-makers within client organizations to understand their business objectives and develop strategic advertising plans
- Sell and manage Tokopedia branding ad solutions to big brands, demonstrating the ability to think strategically about their business and the digital marketing landscape
- Identify opportunities for upper funnel branding objectives, utilizing your knowledge of various digital marketing platforms including Google, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and Tiktok
- Maintain a thorough understanding of digital marketing metrics related to upper-funnel branding objectives
- Collaborate with internal teams to ensure alignment and execution of branding strategies
- Regularly communicate performance metrics, insights, and strategic recommendations to clients, proving the effectiveness of Amazon's branding ad solutions

### What This Role Needs

- Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business, or a related field
- At least 2 years of experience in digital marketing, specifically in a role managing and selling branding ad solutions
- Demonstrated ability to develop strong relationships with top marketing executives
- Strong understanding of digital marketing platforms including Google, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and Tiktok
- Strong analytical skills with the ability to translate data into actionable insights
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills

### Preferred Qualifications

- Previous experience in a role with a focus on ATL advertising and brand marketing
- Demonstrated success in a fast-paced, dynamic, and creative environment
- Experience managing complex projects and multi-tasking with excellent organizational skills
- Proven track record of achieving sales targets and driving sales growth
- In-depth knowledge of Amazon's advertising solutions
- Experience in or deep knowledge of the eCommerce industry
