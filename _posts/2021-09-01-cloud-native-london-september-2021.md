Joining us tonight are some great speakers from  Palo Alto Networks and Helio AG at the September Cloud Native London virtual meetup, from 6:45pm BST tonight, on Rambly and YouTube! 

[Join us on Rambly](https://rambly.app/#/rooms/twitch:cloudnativelon) or catch the Youtube livestream.

### Our agenda for 1 September 2021 is (times in BST):

* 6:45 Kick off
* 7:15 How attackers exploit containerized Azure workloads - and how to secure them (David Okeyode, Palo Alto Networks)
* 7:45 Sustainability, powered by Argo (Christoph Buchli, Helio AG)
* 8:15 Wrap up

We are immensely grateful to [Tecknuovo](https://www.tecknuovo.com/), [Humio](https://humio.com/), [Palo Alto Networks](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/), [CircleCI](https://circleci.com/), and [Netapp](https://www.netapp.com/) for your generous support and sponsorship.
