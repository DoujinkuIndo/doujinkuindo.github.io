Badland Games and DREADLOCKS have just released a demo for DEX,  cyberpunk RPG. The demo is available on PlayStation 4, Xbox One and Steam, and provides codes for free digital items when completed.

The free items are:

* Demo Artbook
* Voice lines
* Wallpapers
* Avatars
* Artworks

Check out the [demo website](http://www.drnilesclinic.com) for more info.

For those interested in the full version of the game, you can purchase it for $19.99 from the following stores:
