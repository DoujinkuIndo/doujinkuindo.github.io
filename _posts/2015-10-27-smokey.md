Smokey was with us from at least August till today (we just dropped him off at the [PAWS adoption center](http://phillypaws.org/locations/adoption-center/)), and he was really the friendliest cat I've ever met.

He'd occassionally get hissed at and swiped at by our permanent cats (fairly standard cat behavior), but he would never attack back. He just loved to sit on our laps, sing little songs, and be a big cow cat. Also, he is instagram famous:
