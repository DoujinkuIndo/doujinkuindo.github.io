* Large container deployments are increasingly common, with 19% of respondents to the 2019 CNCF survey running more than 5000 containers.

* The top challenge when deploying containers is cultural changes with the development team, followed by security, complexity and lack of training.

* Increased release automation is paying off, with 55% of respondents releasing daily or weekly.
