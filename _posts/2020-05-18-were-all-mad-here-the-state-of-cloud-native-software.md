I dress up as the Queen of Hearts to explain how ThredUp, Adidas and the US Air Force have adopted cloud native. A super fun talk as part of Software Cirus' Alice in Wonderland theme.

Summary:
* Containers and Kubernetes are mainstream; More automation drives more frequent releases
* Focus on Day 2 operations; Embrace cultural change
* The rise of DevSecOps; Service mesh is still maturing
