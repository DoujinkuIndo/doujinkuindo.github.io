## Relationship Management

- Departemen: Business Development and Partnerships
- Tingkat: Entry Level
- Lokasi: Indonesia - Jakarta

Tim Business Development dan Partnerships di Shopee sangatlah dinamis dan bersemangat. Tim ini selalu mencari peluang bisnis agar Shopee dapat menjadi platform e-commerce no. 1 di Asia Tenggara dan Taiwan. Tujuan utama dari tim Business Development dan Partnerships adalah untuk menyediakan berbagai variasi produk dengan harga terbaik untuk para pembeli di Shopee.

### Deskripsi Pekerjaan:

- Responsible for establishing and maintaining good working relationships with brands/sellers owners and representatives.
- Resolve sellers-related inquiries, issues, and escalations from various stakeholders.
- Oversee assigned accounts, including negotiating deals, operational improvement and campaign participation to maximize growth.
- Execute tasks related to assigned sellers and Shopee programmes & initiatives and clearly communicate the progress to internal and external stakeholders.
- Achieve targets on various Shopee programmes to generate sales and revenue within schedule.
- Prepare reports on account status and provide in-depth insight on assigned sellers to Team Leads.

### Persyaratan:

- Fresh graduate with Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Economics, Finance, Management or any relevant study.
- Ability to communicate fluently in English (spoken and written).
- Must have excellent communication skills to conduct seller chat via different communication channels.
- Proficiency in using Ms Excel. 
- Logical thinker with strong problem solving skills.
- Highly motivated and target driven.
- Good team player with an aptitude for sales and the ambition to constantly outperform your KPIs.
