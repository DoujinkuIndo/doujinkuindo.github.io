Amber McGregor

I am a wheat hating, design loving, nutella enthusiast from Belfast!

I am twenty-two and Studying IxD at the University of Ulster Belfast. I adore Erik Johansson, Andy Warhol and Ian Wrights work and have an obsession with Street Art. I am trying my best to learn the world of design one book at a time. When I am not reading about the amazing things artists do I am playing with Hama Beads and making protein pancakes.

The career prospect of being the Pink Power Ranger became unrealistic once I turned eighteen, so now I have my heart set on design. You will see Little Elefant appear as my username on most social networks. This is due to the fact that I am tiny yet loud. I am going to start blogging at little-elefant.in, don't be afraid to be nosey.
