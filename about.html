From architect to full-time web developer

Back in 2002 I went to college and studied architecture. During my career as an architect I worked in Amsterdam, Vienna and Frankfurt. I saw some of my projects built. It was fun!

Yet recently I turned my former professional life down to become a full-time web developer.

Nowadays I am still interested in building things, but this time by coding. I also feel inspired by contributing to the growing community of women in tech.
